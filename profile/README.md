## Roar! Fishy Dino is HERE!! 👋
We are a programming team based in South Korea. We have a passion for learning new things and enjoy exploring and developing various programming techniques.

The name Fishydino originated as a playful nickname for a friend on Discord, but it has now become the symbol representing our team.

Fishydino Education Program
In 2022, we conducted a private education program in Russia, focusing on teaching C programming and various other programs to children aged 7 to 10. Currently, the program is no longer active.

Our goal is to share programming knowledge through continuous growth and engage in diverse projects to create innovative solutions. We thrive on sharing ideas with colleagues and embracing challenges for success.

If you have any inquiries, please feel free to contact us. We look forward to working together and seizing opportunities for mutual growth!
